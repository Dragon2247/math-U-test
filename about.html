About the Math UIL Study Tool

The Math UIL Study Tool is a web application designed to help students improve their number sense skills for the University Interscholastic League (UIL) Math competition. The tool generates auto-generated tests and practice quizzes for students to practice their skills and prepare for the competition.

This tool was developed by a team of former UIL Math competitors who wanted to provide a modern and effective way for students to prepare for the competition. We hope that our tool will be helpful for you in your studies and wish you the best of luck in the competition!
